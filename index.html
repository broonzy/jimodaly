I am designing and making Stained Glass for a wide variety of buildings since 1980. From the smallest bespoke lamps, to large church windows, with windows for the home, restaurant and pub in between. I have designed and created glass featuring various styles. I am involved with design projects, glass and mosaic in Ireland, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Belgium, Netherlands, Italy and the U.S.A.
