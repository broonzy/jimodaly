Welcome

Now based in both Ballydehob in West Cork and Cologne Germany,
I have been involved in designing and creating Stained Glass since 1980.
My work is found in homes, churches and establishments throughout Europe and America.

Selection of architectural glass projects:
2004 - 2006 ARC Cologne GmbH, Cologne/Germany, Windows and facade for housing development
2003 Arch. Hans Schilling, Cologne/Germany St.Alexander, Schmallenberg,
Glass installation for church tower
2001 Arch. H. Hohlwein, Much, Germany, Glass ceiling
1999 Arch. J. Schorn, Cologne, Church window design
1995 Concourse, Galway, Ireland - Interior glass screens
1988 Arch. H.J . Lyons, Dublin, Ireland, Glass installation restaurant
